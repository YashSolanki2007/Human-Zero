What is HumanZero?

An AI authenticity checker for the modern web.

HumanZero helps people estimate whether media is AI-generated or manipulated. We provide probabilistic signals — not absolute truth — to help you make more informed decisions about the media you encounter.

What do we do?

Detection built on real research.

01

Detect AI-generated images

Frame-level CLIP-ViT analysis catches spectral and spatial artifacts left by generative models.

02

Detect deepfake / AI video

Uniform frame sampling + temporal majority voting gives robust video-level predictions.

03

Interpretable per-frame results

Every frame gets its own probability score so you can see exactly where signals appear.

04

Confidence tiers

High / Medium / Low confidence labels based on distance from the decision boundary.

Preprocessing
Frames extracted, resized to 224×224, normalised.
Detection model
CLIP ViT-L/14 encoder + finetuned classification head.
Frame scoring
Per-frame sigmoid probabilities averaged to a video score.
Limitations & responsible use
Results are probabilistic signals, not ground truth. Always apply human judgement.
